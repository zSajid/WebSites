Team 10 Advisors

Shamik Sengupta:

Shamik Sengupta is the main advisor for team 10. Dr. Sengupta informs the team of what he wants implemented for NetCity 3D that will help NetCity 3D become a stronger project each step of the way. The members of team 10 have daily meetings with Dr. Sengupta in order to get feedback on the current progress.

Sergio Dascalu:

Sergio Dascalu is the professor for CS 426 during the implementation of NetCity 3D. Sergio Dascalu has team 10 do UML diagrams in order to design NetCity 3D better. In addition, he provides end of the year demo feedback for team 10 to have improvements on user interface, graphical user interface, etc.
